Reading the evidence marks

Backs the claim. This field says something that supports it.

Says nothing. Field has content, but never mentions this capability.

No data at all. Field is empty. Absence of proof, not proof of absence.

Contradicts. This field says the opposite of the claim.

Could not be read. The record itself is damaged. This is our failure, not the facility’s.

How the assessment is decided
  1. Any field could not be read → Could not check.
  2. Any field contradicts the claim → Conflicting evidence.
  3. Fewer than 3 of the 4 fields hold any data → Not enough data.
  4. 3 or more fields back the claim → Strong record support.
  5. Anything else → Limited record support.

Ordered by strength and consistency of the record — not by facility quality.
